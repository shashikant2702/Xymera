About us

Our Story of
Growth and Resilience

Envisioning a healthier and happier society is the sole purpose to which Pradeep Manral and Ramashish Kukreti have dedicated their lives through Xymera Bioscience. These two well-known, self-made, and people-oriented leaders of the industry laid the foundation of this dream organization in 2017.
With over two decades of expertise in oncology care, working with world-class companies and gold-standard products, they set out to create a solution aimed at easing the disease burden, particularly within this segment.
At Xymera Bioscience, the well-being of patients is the core value that drives every strategy, product, and service, ensuring that patient care remains the top priority.

Our Products

Therapeutic Products

Therapeutic medicines are drugs designed to cure, manage, or prevent diseases. They work by targeting disease mechanisms or reducing symptoms, enhancing overall health and quality of life. Prescriptions can be for short-term relief or long-term management based on the condition.

Nutraceutical Products

Nutraceutical care products help cancer patients with the provision of essential vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that help in the proper functioning of the immune system and the general well-being of an individual. They can support the management of treatment side effects and improve recovery.
